Specialist Therapy Centre

At Global Kids Campus, our Therapy Centre is a specialised department designed to support children with developmental, speech, and learning challenges in a structured, compassionate, and highly professional environment.

We integrate Montessori principles with evidence-based therapeutic practices to create a space where children feel comfortable, understood, and motivated to progress. Every programme is carefully planned to meet each child's individual needs.

Our certified therapists work closely with families to ensure consistency between sessions and home life — because true progress happens when children feel supported everywhere they go.

Our Services

1
Speech Therapy

Focused on improving communication skills, articulation, language development, and confidence. Our certified speech therapists work closely with each child to overcome speech delays, stuttering, and comprehension challenges. We use play-based, highly interactive sessions to make learning enjoyable and effective, giving children the tools they need to express themselves clearly and connect with the world around them.

2
ABA Therapy (Applied Behavior Analysis)

Structured behavioral interventions to improve social, communication, and learning skills. Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) helps children develop positive behaviors and reduce challenging ones by breaking down complex tasks into manageable steps. Our dedicated therapists create personalized programs that encourage meaningful progress, fostering independence and helping children thrive in their everyday environments.

3
Educational Therapy

Tailored support for children with learning difficulties, focusing on academic skills, comprehension, and cognitive development. We understand that every child learns differently, which is why we provide individualized strategies to bridge learning gaps. By building foundational skills and boosting academic confidence, we empower children to discover the joy of learning and succeed both inside and outside the classroom.

4
Support for Learning Disabilities

Individualized plans to help children overcome challenges in reading, writing, and understanding concepts. We provide specialized intervention for children with dyslexia, dyscalculia, and other learning differences. Using multi-sensory strategies, we ensure every child receives the compassionate guidance they need to break down barriers, unlock their full potential, and celebrate their unique way of learning.

Our Focus

Certified Therapists

Our team of qualified, experienced therapists specialise in paediatric development. Every child receives expert, compassionate care delivered by professionals who are passionate about making a real difference.

Individualised Plans

No two children are the same. We design personalised therapy programmes based on thorough assessments, ensuring that every goal, activity, and milestone is tailored specifically to each child's unique needs and strengths.

Safe & Calm Environment

Our therapy spaces are thoughtfully designed to be calming, distraction-free, and child-friendly. A secure environment allows children to feel at ease, build trust with their therapist, and focus on meaningful progress.

Parent Partnership

We keep families closely involved through regular progress updates, parent guidance sessions, and open communication. A strong partnership between home and our centre is key to achieving lasting results for every child.

Evidence-Based Methods

All our therapy programmes are grounded in internationally recognised, research-backed methodologies. We continuously update our practices to reflect the latest advances in paediatric therapy and child development science.

Measurable Progress

We set clear, achievable goals and track every child's journey with detailed progress reports. Families can see real, tangible improvement — giving both children and parents the confidence that they are moving in the right direction.

Empowering Every Child

Our aim is to empower every child by building their confidence, enhancing their abilities, and helping them reach their full potential. We believe that with the right guidance, patience, and encouragement, every child can overcome their unique hurdles and discover their true strengths.

At Global Kids Campus Therapy Centre, no challenge is too great. We provide a supportive, inclusive, and joyful environment where children feel safe to learn and grow at their own pace. We walk alongside every child and family with dedication, care, and hope, ensuring that therapy is a positive and transformative journey.
