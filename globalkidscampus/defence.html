Programs Available

Our Defence Branch brings the full Global Kids Campus experience to the heart of the Defence community, offering the same high-quality Montessori-based programs in a warm and welcoming local setting.

Conveniently located for families in Defence and surrounding areas, this branch maintains the same standards of care, learning, and development that parents have come to trust at Global Kids Campus.

All three GKC programs are fully available at our Defence Branch — the same quality and standards you expect from Global Kids Campus.

  • Montessori Daycare — Ages 3 months to 10 years
  • Montessori Preschool — Strong foundations for preschool-age children
  • Therapy Centre — Speech, ABA and educational therapy
